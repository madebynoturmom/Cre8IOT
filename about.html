Who We Are

About Us

Cre8 IOT is an IoT Ecosystem Development and Delivery company. We build ecosystems of connected things that include communication, applications, data, and analytics. The value creation in IoT arises from transforming raw data into actionable insights, enabling businesses to perform actions that add measurable value.

Headquartered in Malaysia, with offices in India and Singapore, our IoT solutions are built on proven use cases that ensure complete delivery, support, and innovation — such as Smart Parking. Our team is composed of industry experts with decades of experience in engineering, operations, and IoT technology. We are committed to delivering innovative solutions that address real-world challenges and create sustainable value for our clients. At Cre8 IOT, we believe in the power of collaboration and co-creation. By working closely with our clients, we ensure that our solutions are tailored to meet their unique needs and objectives.

Our Mission

To offer our clients the best satisfaction in pursuit of providing solutions to embed intelligence in objects, to identify people and things, to connect them to the internet, provide real-time information, process these data and make decisions based on a set of rules; and finally, allow them to be controlled both locally and remotely.

Our Vision

To provide equipment and solutions that will have a significant impact on people’s lives and the communities they live in. To be the “solution provider of choice” by ensuring our customers attain optimal and healthy business performance with the help of our solutions.

Our Purpose

We help public operators and enterprises digitize physical infrastructure, improve service reliability and reduce operational cost and environmental impact.

  • End‑to‑end delivery: devices, connectivity, cloud and apps
  • From data to decisions: actionable dashboards and alerts
  • Security & reliability: multi‑tenant, role‑based access, SLA
