What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI refers to artificial intelligence systems capable of autonomous decision-making, learning, and acting on behalf of users or organizations to achieve specific goals.

  • Autonomous Agents
  • Adaptive Learning
  • Goal-Oriented Intelligence
  • Human-AI Collaboration
